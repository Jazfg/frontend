  • Wednesday is an American coming-of-age comedy, mystery television series based on the character Wednesday Addams by Charles Addams.
    In this show Wednesday Addams is expelled from her school after trying to defend her brother Pugsley. Her parents Gomez and Morticia Addams enroll her at their high school Nevermore Academy; a private school for monstrous outcasts; full of murder mysteries.
    Although many are put off by her cold persona, no one can deny she is force to be reconned with. While playing detective she discovers she has inherited her mother's psychic abilities which allow her to solve a local murder.
